INVISION Biosensor

1st Place - Washington State Science Fair 2025

Designed a low-cost wearable multi-modal biosensor armband for vital sign anomaly detection. Built with ESP32 microcontroller, integrating PPG sensors for heart rate and SpO2 measurement with LEDs, piezo speakers, and thermistors.
